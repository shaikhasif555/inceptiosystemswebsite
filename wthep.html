    UDAYAN GAIDHANI

    Udayan, an engineering and management graduate from BITS, Pilani & IIM Bangalore - India's leading educational institutions - has nearly 35 years of experience in fields as diverse as hospitality, manufacturing, trading, construction, financial systems and others.

    Since his debut in 1981, he has been leading software development and consulting organizations in India, Oman and UAE – to produce and distribute process oriented, integrated systems. His last assignment was with Fiesta Systems – a multi-national organization with operations in multiple countries – as a CEO.

    The current venture reflects his vision and expertise for creating value for all the stake holders. Udayan leads the management team in the capacity of Chief Executive Officer, taking care of product design & development, administration & investor relations.
